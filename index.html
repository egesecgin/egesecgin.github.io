PCB Engraver

We used to produce PCBs with chemical methods for our hobby projects and other competitions at school, but since this way is extremely dangerous, time-consuming and harmful to the environment, I told my automation systems teacher from my school that I wanted to design a PCB engraver, he did really like the idea and we started to plan. Although it took months to calibrate and ensure stable operation, we started to produce a lot of PCB engravers for our school after the first successful prototype.

Monitor that adjusts itself automatically and provides privacy

A monitor I designed to solve the two big problems I was facing, neck pain and the lack of privacy. Monitor can adjust its height itself automatically if I lift my chair or lower it with the help of Arduino, step-motor and an infrared object detection sensor. Its screen can only be viewed with the special glasses, people other than the user see the screen only as a white blank screen.

yoppa.ch

Me and Baran Güneysel (Senior interaction design student at ZHdK) decided to combine some of our ideas and offer a service, so the yoppa brand was formed. Our main goal was to offer our customers quality vegan meals, whose menu changes every day, at a very affordable price. At the same time, we planned to make deliveries in an environment friendly way and to plant a tree in a region that the customer wants every time a meal is ordered. We have not started operations yet, but our plans are ready for implementation.
